Spaghetti Aglio E Olio

This is a simple Italian dish made with very few ingredients. It is a great answer to a tiring day and minimal effort wanted in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 3 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 3-4 Garlic Cloves
  • half a small chilli finely chopped
  • small handful of parsley finely chopped
    •

    Cooking Steps

    1. Bring a large pan of salted water to the boil. Cook the spaghetti for 10 minutes, or until al dente.

    2. When the pasta is almost cooked, heat the oil in a frying pan set over a medium heat and sizzle the garlic and chilli for 1 minute until fragrent and the garlic is lightly golden but not brown.

    3. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the garlicy chilli oil with the parsley. Season and toss to combine. Serve
