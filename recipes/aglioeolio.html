Spaghetti Aglio E Olio

This is a simple Italian dish made with very few ingredients. It is a great answer to a tiring day and minimal effort wanted in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • - 200g Spaghetti
  • - 3 tbsp Olive Oil
  • - 3-4 Garlic Cloves
  • - half a small chilli finely chopped
  • - small handful of parsley finely chopped
