Mustard Chicken Thighs

A delicious family meal using a medlee of fantastic vegetables with honey mustard chicken a must have for any friday night family meal! This uses a slow cooker to get the best result.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • 8 chicken thighs
  • 8 spring onions cut into lengths
  • 150ml chicken stock
  • 1 tbsp dijon mustard
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp double cream
  • 100g frozen peas

Cooking Instructions

  1. Heat the slow cooker. Melt the butter in a frying pan and brown the chicken thighs all over. Ensure the skins get a lot of colour. Season and put into the slow cooker.

  2. Brown the spring onions and add them to the slow cooker. Add the stock, mustard and honey. Cook on low for 4 hours.

  3. Stir in the cream and peas then cook for a further 15 minutes with the lid off. You can recrisp the chicken skins under a grill if you like.
